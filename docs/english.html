In Brief

Lärkstadens studiecentrum is a study center that also serves as a residence for college men.

It is located in central Stockholm close to all the main universities and colleges in the city. In addition offering access to study rooms, activities include guest speakers from a wide variety of professions, talks and classes of a cultural nature, sports and excursions.

For its religious orientation, the center is affiliated with the Prelature of Opus Dei, an organization in the Catholic Church.

For more information about the activities of the study center, get in touch with us by email. If you are interested in applying for a room, fill in the application form below.
